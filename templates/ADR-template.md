## Context

The forces at play: the problem, the constraints (technical, product, time), and
what made the decision necessary *now*. Include the evidence (measurements,
research, an incident) if there is any — an ADR is more durable when it records
*why*, not just *what*.

## Decision

What was decided, stated plainly. If there are triggers/conditions, list them.

## Consequences

What becomes easier, harder, or riskier as a result — including the costs you are
deliberately accepting and any follow-on work this creates. Be honest about the
downside.
